BACKGROUND
EDUCATION/WORK

My post high-school academic journey commenced with a Bachelor's degree in Spanish, pursued at Delhi University, the interest for learning languages led me to understand the world in various different ways, making me want to explore life in different ways. Following my undergraduate studies, I delved deeper into the intricacies of journalism, undertaking a Diploma in English Journalism at the prestigious Indian Institute of Mass Communication in Delhi, India. This period allowed me to refine my communication skills and gain a comprehensive understanding of the principles underpinning journalistic integrity, which eventually led me to the role of Copy Editor at Times of India, a forerunner publication under Bennett & Coleman Pvt Ltd, located in Noida, India. I have honed my editorial prowess and contributed to the realm of media and journalism, getting a better understanding of how important it is to maintain the truthfulness of stories and information, safeguarding its original form and still being able convey the right message to the audience without fabricating it, moulding the narrative according to the story and not vice versa. Eager to expand my horizons and embrace the artistry of visual storytelling, I further embarked on a truly transformative academic pursuit, a Master’s degree in Cinematography at The Film and TV School of the Academy of Performing Arts in Prague (FAMU). This immersive program equipped me with technical expertise, creative vision, and a nuanced understanding of cinematographic techniques. My educational journey, coupled with my practical experiences is nothing but the fruit of the expansive knowledge I was fortunate enough to receive in different domains.

    • CAMERA SUPERVISOR/PHOTOGRAPHER

    I have been working as a Camera Supervisor with FAMU International, Prague since 2021. Bachelor students from NYU TISCH, YALE, COLUMBIA and other universities come for short-term summer/winter programs at FAMU every year, where they are introduced to the practical experience and knowledge of filmmaking and more importantly shooting on 35mm and 16mm film stock. My job as a supervior is to conduct technical workshops (camera,lighting,grip), consultations on storyboarding, colorgrading and help students prepare their final films at FAMU.
