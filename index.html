SILENCE IN MIRACLES (TEASER)
COMING SOON (JUNE 2024)

Constance believes. A solid belief in her monastic vocation, in her artistic talent, but above all in the faith that Charlotte, her endeared mentor, an old Carmelite nun dying of exhaustion, can still be saved, and must still be saved. But her superiors seem to believe differently. When they ask Constance to take over Charlotte’s artistic duties, and to paint her portrait, Constance starts an inevitable dive into self-doubt and defiance.

THE LAST MEATING

In a short dramatic horror film, a young man goes to visit a girl with flowers but the encounter takes a different turn. Weaving the story in black and white photography, shot on Kodak Double X 5222 35mm film stock, the unsettling atmosphere unfolds itself, revealing the true motives of the girl he goes to visit.

THE HOLE

An athlete on a lower floor tries to do his morning exercise. But he is disturbed by the sounds of the drummer coming from the apartment above and the flakes of ceiling dust that is falling on him. Drenched in dust and plaster splatter, the athlete grabs a broom and punches a hole in the ceiling. The drummer above amused by the extreme measure, engages with him in an erotic game of push and pull of the broom.
